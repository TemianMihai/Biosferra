It's time to get healthy!

This website is ment to be used by busy people who want their fruits and vegetables right infront of their houses.

How does it works?
Create a post

Producers will create a post. They will complete fields with a name, price, delivery time and a short description.

Delivery

They will create a basket with fruits or vegetables from offer and our partners will take care of delivery.

Get paid

You will get paid when the basket will arrive to the reciever. Or people will pay their basket if they come to get it from you.

How does this website works?

Biosphera is a service that has the purpose to help people with their selling. Producers of vegetables or fruits will post their offer and people who want to eat healthy but don't have enough time to go to the market will find the perfect pack here. It's an easier way for producers to promote their goods and a faster way for other people to find healthy vegetables or fruits.

About our producers

Our service wants to help independent producers to promote their goods. To use this service they have to agree a document that say any user can visit them any time. So if you are near a seller and want to see what you are going to eat you can go to his house and check his product.
